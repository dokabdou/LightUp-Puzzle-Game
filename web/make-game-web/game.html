LIGHTUP THE UNIVERSE

By Gonzalez Diallo Genebes Chettouane
Sounds and music from Zapsplat.com
Original "Light Up" game

> RULES <

🌌 The universe is dark... put baby suns to light it up, but never two facing each other ! 🌌

🌞 You can put and remove a baby sun with a left-mouse click 🌞

🛰️ You can put and remove a mark with a right-mouse click, they are just indicators 🛰️

🌑 Asteroids are walls with special rules : 🌑

  • A group of many small asteroids represents a simple wall
  • A big white asteroid must not have baby suns next to it
  • Other asteroids are grouped by 1, 2, 3 or 4 and must have their exact number of baby suns next to them

❌ An inverted baby sun or asteroid means that it has an error ❌

🕹️ There are five options with the buttons under the grid : 🕹️

  • "RESTART" to restart the current game
  • "SOLVE" to show its solution
  • "UNDO" to cancel your last moves
  • "REDO" to redo your cancelled moves
  • "NEW GAME" to create a random game with your chosen options

💫 The wrapping option makes the game connected by its edges, you can activate it by clicking "WRAPPING" then "NEW GAME" 💫

🔳 You can also choose to always have a square game with the "SQUARE" checkbox 🔳

📊 Change the percentage of walls you want for your game with the drop-down list 📊
